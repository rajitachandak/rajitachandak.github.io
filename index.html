About

I am a PhD candidate in the Operations Research & Financial Engineering (ORFE) department at Princeton University, where I am advised by Matias Cattaneo. My research interests include mathematical statistics, theoretical machine learning and econometrics.

I am very excited to be joining the Institute of Mathematics at École Polytechnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL) as a Bernoulli Instructor this summer!

Background

I received my MA in ORFE from Princeton in 2021. Prior to starting at Princeton, I completed my Bachelors of Science with Honors in Applied Mathematics and Economics at Brown University in 2019. I wrote my honors thesis on Energy Aware Optimization of Scalable Load Balancing Strategies under the supervision of Kavita Ramanan.
